About Us

Welcome to HightView English High School!

HighView is a public school located in [nallasopara, Maharashtra]. We are a diverse community of learners who are dedicated to academic excellence and personal growth. We offer a rigorous academic program that prepares students for success in college and beyond. We also offer a variety of extracurricular activities to help students explore their interests and develop their talents.


Our school's mission is to provide all students with a high-quality education that prepares them for success in college and career. We believe that all students can learn and achieve, and we are committed to providing them with the support they need to reach their full potential.

Our History

HightView was founded in 1999 by a group of community members who were passionate about education. The school has grown significantly over the years, and we now serve over 2000 students.
